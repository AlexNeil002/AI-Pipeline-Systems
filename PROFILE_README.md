# Alex Neil

**AI Pipeline Systems Builder**

I build modular AI data pipelines for small and medium businesses — turning raw operational data into daily decisions owners can actually act on.

---

## What I Build

Most SMBs are sitting on valuable data they never look at. I connect that data to an AI layer and surface the insights that matter: what sold, what's stagnating, who's at risk of churning, where margin is being lost.

The client gets a Notion dashboard and a daily briefing. No raw data. No dashboards they need a data degree to read.
